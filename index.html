Week 1 Note

Your lungs are ready from running — your sit bones aren't. Saddle soreness is normal for 5–7 days. Use chamois cream, keep cadence 85–95 rpm, and stay in Z2. The optional 60 min Friday is just extra Z2 — not harder, just longer.

The One Rule for Race Day

Start in Z2. The first 30km will feel embarrassingly easy. That's exactly right. Every watt saved in the first half is a watt available when it matters — km 70–100. The riders who go out hard always crack. You won't.
